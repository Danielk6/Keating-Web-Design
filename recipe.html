Hummus Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb dried Chickpeas
  • 1 cup Tahini
  • 3 Lemons, juiced
  • 3 cloves Garlic, mashed
  • 4 tsp Cumin
  • 1 cup Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp Sea Salt

Directions

  1. Soak chickpeas overnight (or 8 hours), water should be 1-2 inches above
  2. Drain and rinse beans
  3. Optional: remove skins for smoother texture
  4. Bring 4 cups of water for every 1 cup of chickpeas to a boil (chickpeas already in water)
  5. Reduce heat and simmer about 2 hours
  6. Retaining liquid, drain chickpeas
  7. In a food processor, add the chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, cumin, salt, and olive oil
  8. Add the retained liquid (recommended to use half) to make the hummus creamier
